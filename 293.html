Description

After looking at tickets #290, #291 and #292, make a list of ways it might make sense to integrate OMERO support into CellProfiler and CellProfiler Analyst.

For example, CP could operate on a screen stored in OMERO, saving segmentation results to the DB. Then CPA could obtain thumbnails from the OMERO server, and store the resultant classification back to OMERO using Omero Tables.

This is in part of our effort to research potential integration of OMERO with CellProfiler Analyst and other (Python-based) CP code.
