Concept of set of derived (i.e. fitted) images

Reported by: aivar Owned by: aivar
Priority: major Milestone: imagej2-b8-analysis
Component: Other Version:
Severity: serious Keywords:
Cc: Blocked By:
Blocking: #1808, #1815

Description

In SLIM Plugin the fitting process takes an image with a Lifetime dimension and produces several fitted images that represent different qualities of the image.

It would be useful to have a concept of a workspace that holds the original and fitted images.

Also useful would be some way of linking the images and modeling the fitting process.
