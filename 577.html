comment:1 Changed 2011-06-09T13:30:34-05:00 by gharris

What keys to use for what action?

ImageJ uses forward and backward with '<' and '>' with

  • No-modifier for channels
  • Ctrl-modifier for slices
  • Alt-modifier to frames
